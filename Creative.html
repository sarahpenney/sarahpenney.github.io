Creative Emails

I love to push whats possible in Email Design and Development and this is an example of just how fun you can make them.

This artist already has a big visual identity, so I used the same gifs they created for their website, and converted them into an exciting Email design for their online shop.

Emails like this are not going to suit most brands, especially because usually the copy is the most important thing to get across, and would be hard to recreate within a smaller scope, but it is a good concept for possibly one off exciting campaigns for those special occasions.
